Cloud Security Audit — Healthcare SaaS

Client: Healthcare SaaS Platform (HIPAA-regulated)
Role: Cloud Security Architect
Duration: 4 weeks
High
Problem

Case Study 03

Incident Response — Ransomware Recovery

Client: Mid-Market Manufacturing Company
Role: Incident Response Lead
Duration: 72 hours (containment) + 2 weeks (recovery)
Critical
Problem

Manufacturing company hit by ransomware attack that encrypted 80% of their file servers, disrupted production systems, and demanded $2M Bitcoin ransom. Business operations completely halted.

Solution

Led emergency incident response team. Isolated affected systems within 4 hours to prevent lateral movement. Conducted forensic analysis to identify attack vector (phishing email → compromised VPN credentials → lateral movement → domain admin → ransomware deployment). Recovered 100% of data from offline backups. Implemented security improvements.

  • Incident Timeline & Root Cause Analysis
  • Forensic Evidence Preservation

Assessment Process

Every engagement follows a proven four-phase methodology ensuring thorough coverage and actionable results.

01

Reconnaissance

OSINT gathering, network mapping, attack surface discovery, technology fingerprinting, and passive information collection.

02

Enumeration

Service fingerprinting, vulnerability scanning, manual probing, authentication testing, and configuration analysis.

03

Exploitation

Controlled exploitation, privilege escalation, lateral movement, data exfiltration testing, and business logic abuse.

04

Reporting

Executive summary, technical details with PoC, risk-rated findings, prioritized remediation roadmap, and retesting.
